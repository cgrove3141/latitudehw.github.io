Temperature vs Latitude

A Scatterplot of the max temperatures of a city against their latitude

We can see a significant correlation between latitude and temperature, cities further away from the equator (0) decrease sharply in temperature, though interestingly the drop seems to be less for the cities in the southern hemisphere, though this may be related to the time of year the data was taken
